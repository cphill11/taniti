Frequently Asked Questions Content

Currencies:

  • Taniti uses USD as currency, but many shops accept euros and yen.
  • Currencies are able to be exchanged at Taniti banks.
  • Many businesses accept major credit cards.

Packing:

  • Taniti outlets are 120volt power outlets (same as U.S.)

Drinking considerations:

  • Legal drinking age is 18 years, but this is not strictly enforced.
  • Alcohol is not served in the hours between midnight and 9a.m.

Holidays:

  • Taniti enjoys many holidays.
  • Many tourist attractions & restaurants are closed on holidays.

Healthcare:

  • Taniti has 1 large hospital with many multilingual employees.
  • Several clinics for your convenience.

Safety:

  • Violent crime is rare on Taniti.
  • Petty crimes (such as pickpocketing) are starting to increase as tourism increases.

Many younger Tanitians speak English. Minimal English spoken rurally, especially by older residents.
